Who is IDAS Engineering?

IDAS Engineering Inc. is dedicated to advancing the technology for implementing control of hydraulic machines by electronic means. Applicability includes, but is not limited to, motion control, pressure control and force control. Both theory and practical application are the tools of IDAS Engineering Inc. No other technical team is as well versed or experienced in the nuances, peculiarities and design measures needed to most optimally and quickly apply electrohydraulic motion control technology required of the most modern servo and proportional valves and pump controls.

IDAS Engineering Inc. is a family-owned enterprise. Jack L. Johnson, the patriarch and chief executive, whose technical expertise is electrohydraulics and electronic control of hydraulic machines, is a graduate Electrical Engineer from Milwaukee School of Engineering and is a registered professional engineer. He has more than 30 years of experience in the design, commissioning and teaching of complex electrohydraulic systems.
